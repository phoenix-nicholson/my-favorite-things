A little about Xiao Long Bao

This is a Xiao Long Bao or a Soup Dumplings. Xiao Long Bao are like regular dumplings but they have a rich pork or shrimp stew inside of them.

Why they are so good

  • They are full of rich fatty flavors.
  • They are light but fufilling.
  • There are a lot of different types of Xiao Long Bao.
